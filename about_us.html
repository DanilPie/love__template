' The best love story ever '

(1) Our acquaintance

I remember your first like on my photo on Instagram and I then liked you back,
because you are beautiful (and it didn't seem to me). I didn't know what to write,
and in the hope that you will answer, I posted a cute story with Vanya. You wrote
to me when I was walking in the woods and I immediately replied, I remember that
I was happy and we immediately struck up a conversation. We chatted for about
two weeks (I don’t remember exactly, but it seems like that), and then we decided
to meet.

(2) First impression

Before our meeting, I was very worried, to be honest. I really liked you
from the photos and in and I wanted to make a good impression.
I bought blueberries, because, at that time, you already told
me that you are celebrating it. And now I see you ...
Many may have told you that you are more beautiful in life than
in the photo, well, so as not to repeat them and be honest
with you, I will say that you are not only more beautiful, but also super
good and honey

(3) First walk

I met so many different people every single day and have so many friends over
the years, but meeting you for the first time was something special, the vibe,
the force and the feeling was different. It was more unique and special. Your for-
ce of attraction was super strong, which immediately pulled me more closer
to you.

(4) Just frends

I remember too bad the days when we were just friends, but you were
always very kind and considerate to me. For a long time I did not
understand this and did not give value to your efforts. Most likely,
simply because I was very stupid or because I have never had such a
friendship with anyone. I am very grateful to fate that we passed this
stage and soon became not only teaching friends, but also soulmates.

(5) Soulmates

Now sitting here and typing, I realized that for me personally, we have moved to
the level of "more than just friends" from January 8, 2021.The day we went
for a walk with your youngest sister. That day I met your mother and for the
first time I felt something like sympathy for you. Since then, my life has become
much better, because I always have a person who will support, listen and, if
necessary, give advice. I have become very attached to you from that moment.

(6) WE LOVE EACH OTHER

10/28/2021 the happiest period in my life began. I don’t understand
how I didn’t feel and didn’t notice feelings for you before. Apparently,
everything has its time. Every day,when I wake up, I think about you,
take the phone to write you good morning, darling. More than any-
thing, I'm afraid of losing you. I hope that our relationship is exactly
that fairy tale-exception that will last forever. I love and accept you
for who you are and I want us to become better only next to each
other.
